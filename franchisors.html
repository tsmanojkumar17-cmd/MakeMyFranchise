Brand Acquisition Strategy

Brand Onboarding Process

Three phases of brand partnership designed to build credibility, demonstrate value and create long-term franchise success.

Phase 1

Early Stage — Build Case Studies

Focus on emerging brands, regional successes and expanding chains. We offer faster reach, structured investor filtering and lower acquisition cost while building your track record.

Phase 2

Growth Stage — Use Data

Approach mid-size and national brands with proven results. We pitch with data: qualified investors, higher closure ratios and verified success stories from Phase 1 partnerships.

Phase 3

Leadership Stage — Brands Approach You

Top brands seek us out because we deliver qualified investors. At this stage, you have the leverage to be selective about which brands you represent on behalf of investors.
