Everything you need to know about franchise investing and our advisory process

MakeMyFranchise is a specialized franchise advisory firm that helps investors find the right franchise opportunities and helps brands scale through franchising. We provide franchise advisory & matchmaking, franchisor growth consulting, investment feasibility & ROI analysis, legal & operational support, and location & market planning. Our approach is consultative and data-driven — we recommend what fits you, not the market trend.

Our services are designed for two groups: Franchise Investors — first-time entrepreneurs, professionals seeking side income, families entering business, or investors testing entrepreneurship; and Franchisors — brands looking to expand their network with qualified franchise partners across new markets and cities in India.

Our process is simple: (1) Complete your profile — we understand your goals, experience, and investment range. (2) We create a personalized shortlist of vetted franchise brands. (3) Deep evaluation with market insights, ROI expectations, and risk mapping. (4) Direct brand introductions to explore franchise models. (5) Deal support from negotiation to documentation. (6) Launch & beyond — rollout planning and ongoing support.
