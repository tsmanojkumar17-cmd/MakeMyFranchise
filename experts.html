How the Expert Programme Works

From application to earning — a clear, simple path to becoming part of India’s premier franchise expert network.

01

Apply

Submit a brief application sharing your domain expertise, sector experience, professional background and what type of sessions you can conduct (seminars, webinars, workshops, advisory).

02

Profile Review

Our team reviews your application and domain expertise. We assess your knowledge depth, communication ability and relevance to our audience of franchise investors and entrepreneurs.

04

05

Engage & Earn

Lead sessions as and when they are scheduled or requested. Earn your commission after each engagement. The more you participate, the more you build reputation and income.

06

Grow Together

As the platform scales, so do your opportunities. Top-performing experts get featured prominently, invited to flagship events, and offered higher-value advisory engagements.
